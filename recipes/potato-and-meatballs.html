Mashed Potatos and Meatballs

Ingredients

Meatballs
  • Mince
  • Onion
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Milk
  • Salt And Pepper
  • Salt And Pepper
Mash
  • Potatos
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt and Pepper
Brown Sause
  • Beef stock
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Black currant Gel
  • Cream
  • optional soy sause

Description

Meatballs
  1. Turn on the oven at 150 deg centigrade
  2. Line Oven Tray with Parchment paper
  3. But breadcrumbs in larg bowl and add milk and season well with salt and pepper, let swell
  4. Grate the Onion
  5. Add mince and onion to the bowl
  6. Mix until everyting is incorporated
  7. Make balls and put them on the oven tray
  8. Put oven tray in oven and bake for 12 minutes
  9. Heat a frypan on medium-high heat
  10. Add butter and fry the meatballs to get color
Brown Sause
  1. In the pan where you fried the meatballs, add a bit more butter and some flour
  2. Add beef stock and simmer until thickened stiring occationaly
  3. Add cream
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. If sause looks a bit to pale, add a couple of dashes of soy sause
Mash
  1. In a pot boil pealed potatos in salted water
  2. Drain and mash the potatoes
  3. Melt butter and add milk into a separate pan to heat up
  4. Add milk and butter mixture a little at a time to potatoes until good texture
