Lasagna

Ingredients

Mince Sause
  • Mince
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomato Pure
Bechamelle Sause
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Grated Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
Assembling

Lasanga Plates

Description

Mince Sause
  1. Chop the onions
  2. Grate or crush garlic
  3. Fry mince in hot pan until nice color
  4. Add onions and Garlic and fry a bit more
  5. Add tomato Paste and let fry for 1 minute
  6. Add Chopped Tomatoes and season with salt and pepper
Bechamelle Sause
  1. Melt Butter in a pan
  2. Add flour
  3. Mix and let fry for a minute
  4. Add milk and heat while stiring until thickend
  5. Add grated cheese and season with salt and pepper
Assemble
  1. Turn on the oven fan forced
  2. Layer Lasanga in a oven dish. Start with Bechamelle, then Mince and then Lasanga Plates, repeat for as many layers as you would like.
  3. Put in oven until done
